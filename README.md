#  Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

# Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** to analyze sales, profit, discount impact, and regional performance using the Superstore dataset.

The dashboard helps identify:

* Profit-driving segments
* Loss-making categories
* Discount impact on business
* Regional and yearly trends

---

# Business Problem

Businesses often increase discounts to boost sales, but:
 Are discounts actually increasing profit?
 Which categories are causing losses?
 Which regions perform best?

This dashboard answers these critical business questions.

---

#  Key Dashboard Pages

#  1. Executive Dashboard

* **Total Sales:** 2.3M
* **Total Profit:** 286.4K
* **Profit Margin:** 12.5%

# Insights:

* Technology category generates highest profit
* Furniture category has very low profitability
* Some sub-categories (like Tables) are loss-making

---

# 2. Regional Analysis (Map View)

* Interactive map showing profit distribution across states
* **Highest Profit Region:** West
* **Top State:** California

# Insights:

* West region is the strongest contributor
* Profit concentration is uneven across states

---

#  3. Discount Impact Analysis

* Visual showing **Profit vs Discount levels**

# Insights:

* Higher discounts lead to **negative profit**
* Heavy discounting is a major reason for losses
* Low/no discount gives maximum profit

---

# 4. Sales & Profit Trend (Year-wise)

* Line chart showing yearly growth

# Insights:

* Sales dipped in **2015**, then recovered strongly
* Overall business shows upward trend
* Profit growth is slower compared to sales

---

# 5. Customer Segment Analysis

* Consumer, Corporate, Home Office comparison

#### Insights:

* **Consumer segment** generates highest profit (~46.8%)
* Home Office segment performs weakest

---

# Key Insights Summary

*  High discounts are directly reducing profitability
*  Certain products (like Tables) are consistently loss-making
*  Technology is the most profitable category
*  West region dominates profit contribution
*  Sales and profit show long-term growth trend
